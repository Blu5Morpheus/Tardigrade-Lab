## Active research

**VQE × LIGO classifier.** Variational quantum eigensolver classifier
for binary-merger gravitational waves. Trained on real superconducting
hardware (IBM Quantum's ibm_nairobi), targeting discrimination between
true chirps and glitch artefacts in O3 strain data. Active collaboration
with a UCSB PhD candidate; arXiv preprint in preparation.

**Clifford geometric algebra agents.** Cl(3,0) and Cl(3,1) equivariant
neural architectures with rotation, boost, and reflection symmetries
native to the network rather than imposed by loss-function constraints.
Released as the open-source `tardigrade_agent` Python package, with
plasma instability control via BOUT++ as the initial application
target.

**Topological knot invariant cryptography.** Concept paper exploring
knot invariants as the basis for post-quantum cryptographic primitives.
Speculative; in early drafting.

**Lattice QFT surrogates for amplituhedron volume estimation.** Combining
lattice gauge theory ML surrogates with positive-geometry sampling —
exploratory work presented at a LIGO convention.
